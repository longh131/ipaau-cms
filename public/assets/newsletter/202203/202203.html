Continuing Professional Development (CPD)

All members are required to comply with the CPD requirements of Pronouncement 7 and complete a minimum of 120 hours CPD over each three year period with a minimum of 20 hours for each new CPD competency area. The IPA offers a broad range of CPD solutions which can be found here. The new Pronouncement 7 will not be enforced until 2024 when the next CPD cycle commences, however, we encourage members to consider and apply the new requirements. More information will be released on an ongoing basis.

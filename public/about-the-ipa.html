About Us

For over 100 years, the IPA has been providing a community where accountants come together, learn, and grow. We’re on a mission to build the most supportive professional development community for public accountants and small businesses alike. We recognise the vital contribution that small business makes to the economy globally. As trusted advisers, IPA members play a pivotal role in the lives of their small business clients and are in an ideal position to support the productivity, growth and prosperity of small business, making a genuine economic contribution. 

"For every small business

to have one of

our members by their side" 

Who We Are

At the Institute of Public Accountants, we prioritise ensuring our members never feel like just a number. Our focus is on providing practical resources and services that genuinely support them. We do not use call centres, and our CEO personally contacts new members—a small gesture that reflects who we are as an organisation.

We also place strong emphasis on mental health, offering education and training resources to support member wellbeing. At the same time, we remain committed to helping members guide their small business clients through challenging periods. Many local Australian businesses rely on the support of an IPA member—now more than ever.

Our Commitment To Members

IPA is committed to supporting members through high-quality continuing professional development and education pathways, helping them remain current and relevant in an increasingly competitive and evolving market.

This is supported by a range of communications, including technical newsletters and updates, major conferences and networking opportunities, and an award-winning online technical support platform.

Advocating on behalf of members is an ongoing priority for the IPA, and we have a seat at more than 120 forums with key political, regulatory and industry stakeholders across Australia and overseas. This work is reinforced with a multitude of formal submissions to key influencers and an active media program.

 The IPA also delivers many products and services to benefit members and their businesses through an array of strategic commercial partnerships.

Global Designation 

The IPA Group is the world’s largest SME/SMP professional accountancy group, representing and supporting accountants globally. The Group brings together the Institute of Financial Accountants (UK), the Institute of Public Accountants – International, and the Association of Accounting Technicians Australia (AAT), which joined in 2020 to further strengthen our SME focus.

We are a full member of the International Federation of Accountants (IFAC) and the Confederation of Asian and Pacific Accountants (CAPA), uniting leading accounting bodies to enhance advocacy, professional recognition, and international opportunities for our members worldwide.

Research & Advocacy

The IPA leads research and advocacy to represent small businesses and SME accountants, shaping policy through evidence-based insights. This includes the Small Business White Paper, which amplifies the sector’s voice, influences government reform, and promotes practical solutions that support sustainable growth and stronger communities.

Delivering Education

The IPA delivers high-quality education through its own global programs and strategic partnerships with universities in Australia and internationally. These pathways support lifelong learning, professional recognition, and career progression, equipping members and students with practical, future-focused skills for a rapidly evolving accounting profession.
